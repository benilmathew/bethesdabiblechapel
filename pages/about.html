Who We Are

Bethesda Bible Chapel is a vibrant community of believers committed to knowing God, growing in faith, and serving others. We welcome people from all walks of life to join us in worship, fellowship, and service.

Our chapel was founded with a vision to create a place where people could experience God's love, grow in their relationship with Christ, and make a difference in the world around them.
